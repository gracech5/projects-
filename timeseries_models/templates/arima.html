ARIMA Forecast

Actual growth values below are calculated based on the Total Trade cumulative year-on-year growth rates

For example: Q2 actual growth rates are calculated using Jan to Jun values over the corresponding Jan to Jun period in the previous year

Forecasted growth values below are calculated based on full year forecasted values.

For example: Q2 forecasted growth rates are calculated based on the ARIMA model that was trained using Q1 data and before. The predictions are made for the entire year ahead. Forecasted growth rate for Q2 would be: actual Q1 + predicted Q2,Q3 & Q4 values over the values in the prev year.
